The Two Upgrade Paths — How They Relate
There are actually two ways to upgrade soldier stats, which is fine but not explained to the player:

Path A — Blacksmith Upgrade button:
Pay from your inventory → tier advances linearly (wood → stone → iron → gold → diamond → netherite) → buff applied instantly. Fast and simple.

Path B — Craft items → Deposit to Armory → Equip:
You craft the swords/armor yourself (or get them from loot), deposit them, then equip. Uses physical items as cost instead of the blacksmith material cost. Gives the player a choice of path. Also works correctly.

These two paths coexist fine — whichever sets a higher tier wins, so you can't accidentally downgrade.
